Missouri DESE School Finance

Finance staff at 500+ districts used a 2003-era system — 4+ hours per submission, no error guidance. Research with 12 finance directors revealed the core issue wasn't the UI but the submission mental model. Districts thought in fiscal years; the system was structured by data field type. Restructuring IA around that insight drove the 40–50% time reduction.

How I Work

From ambiguity to shipped

Senior UX isn't about making things pretty. It's about making the right call under constraint, bringing stakeholders with you, and delivering work that earns trust.

01
Discover & Frame

Before designing, I need to understand the actual problem — not the stated one. Stakeholder interviews, contextual inquiry, heuristic audits. Most redesigns fail because they solve the symptom. I find the system.

Stakeholder interviewsContextual inquiryHeuristic audit
02
Research & Synthesise

User interviews, usability testing, and synthesis sessions produce not just insights but hypotheses. Every design decision maps back to evidence, not instinct.

User interviewsUsability testingAffinity mapping
03
Design & Validate

IA and flows first, lo-fi to validate structure, hi-fi to validate hierarchy and interactions. The most important design decision is often which option not to pursue.

WireframingFigma prototypesAxure RP 10
04
Build & Ship

My front-end background means I work directly with engineering. The design that ships is the one that matters. Everything before it is just a hypothesis.

Design systemsDev handoffWCAG 2.1 AA
