About

I’m Shivani Harode, a Senior UI/UX Designer based in Frisco, TX with 8+ years of experience building complex enterprise and government systems from the ground up.

My work lives at the intersection of rigorous research, accessible design, and engineering realities. I’ve spent the last several years transforming legacy government applications into modern, efficient tools — the kind that caseworkers and district administrators actually want to use.
