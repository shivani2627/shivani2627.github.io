About Me

Designer, Artist & Problem Solver

I'm a UX/UI designer and artist passionate about solving complex problems through thoughtful design. Right now, I'm working with the State of Missouri on the DESE School Finance Application, transforming how 500+ school districts manage their finances.

Previously, I was the solo UI/UX designer with the State of Wisconsin on the PROTECT Case Management System — handling five major design efforts: logo design, desktop app redesign, Details Pane web feature, XCQ legacy web redesign, and the eFiling application.

Before that, I designed 30+ screens at HP – Managed Print Services, self-teaching Axure RP 10 to meet project needs and delivering on schedule. I've also worked at Atos|Syntel and Synechron, delivering results for FedEx and Citibank.

I hold a degree in Information Technology from Yeshwantrao Chavan College of Engineering in India, where I discovered my passion for human-centered design.

🏆  Outside of work, I love old Bollywood music — nothing like Lata or Kishore. I recently won the Best Creative Dish award at the Dallas StarChef culinary competition presented by Michelin-star chef Mayank Istwal. Creativity truly transcends mediums.
