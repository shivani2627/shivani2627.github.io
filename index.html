How I Work

From ambiguity to shipped

Senior UX isn't about making things pretty. It's about making the right call under constraint, bringing stakeholders with you, and delivering work that earns trust.

01
Discover & Frame

Before designing, I understand the actual problem — not the stated one. Stakeholder interviews, contextual inquiry, heuristic audits. Most redesigns fail because they solve the symptom. I find the system.

Stakeholder interviewsContextual inquiryHeuristic audit
02
Research & Synthesise

User interviews, usability testing, and synthesis sessions produce not just insights but hypotheses — testable assumptions about what a better experience looks like.

User interviewsUsability testingAffinity mapping
03
Design & Validate

IA and flows first, lo-fi to validate structure, hi-fi to validate hierarchy and interactions. The most important decision is often which option not to pursue.

WireframingFigma prototypesAxure RP 10
04
Build & Ship

My front-end background means I work directly with engineering. The design that ships is the one that matters. Everything before it is just a hypothesis.

Design systemsDev handoffWCAG 2.1 AA
