About Me

I'm a UX/UI designer and artist passionate about solving complex problems through thoughtful design. Right now, I'm working with the State of Missouri on the DESE School Finance Application, transforming how 500+ school districts manage their finances.

Previously, I was the solo UI/UX designer with the State of Wisconsin on the PROTECT Case Management System. I handled five major design efforts: logo design, desktop app revamp, details pane web app, XCQ legacy web redesign, and the eFiling application.

Before that, I designed 30+ screens at HP – Managed Print Services, where I self-taught Axure RP 10 to meet project needs and delivered on schedule. I've also worked at major IT firms like Atos|Syntel and Synechron, delivering results for clients like FedEx and Citibank.

I hold a degree in Information Technology from Yeshwantrao Chavan College of Engineering in India. During my internship, I discovered my passion for human-centered design, which became the foundation of my thesis project and career.

Outside of work, I'm a huge fan of old Bollywood music (there's nothing like Lata or Kishore). I love experimenting in the kitchen, and recently won the Best Creative Dish award at the Dallas StarChef culinary competition presented by Michelin star chef Mayank Istwal. Creativity truly transcends mediums.
